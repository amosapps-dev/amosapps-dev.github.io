About

I’m a solo builder exploring ideas through small, playful experiments. I use apps as a way to test concepts, behaviours, and interactions rather than chase polished outcomes. This page is a snapshot of that work and how I approach building things end to end.

Projects

What thing am I today?

An exploration of labels, identity, and interpretation. Each day, the app assigns a single, loosely defined “thing” to the user. The experience is minimal by design, inviting users to notice how easily they project context, humour, or personal significance onto a simple prompt.
