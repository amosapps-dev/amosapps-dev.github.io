What thing am I today?

What thing am I today? is a simple iOS app that assigns you a different “thing” each day. Rather than offering explanations or categories, it treats labels as prompts—inviting users to reflect on how easily meaning is projected onto vague definitions. The ambiguity is intentional, leaving space for interpretation rather than instruction, and turning a small daily result into something personal, humorous, or quietly reflective.
